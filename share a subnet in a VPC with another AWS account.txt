share a subnet in a VPC with another AWS account

Logging to the Master Account

1) Create a Custom vpc in the region
2) Open the RAM manager
3) Go to settings ----> click Enable sharing within your AWS Orginizations
4) Go to shared by me ---> Resource share
5) Type the name = resourceshare
6) In the Allow external accounts ---> give the Account id of the participant account
7) Click the and select the resource share ----> click Modify
8) Select the subnets and save changes
9) Go to vpc of and select the subnet and click actions and share subnet and select the Resource name
10) Go to participant account and check the subnets are shared
11) Go the RAM Console in the participant account and see shared with me and click shared resources
